How recipients are selected

A person is included if ALL of the following are true:

  1. Enabled = true in people.csv
  2. Role = SUPER or USER in people.csv (all other roles are excluded)
  3. They have at least one building in exportUsers.csv whose state/region matches one of the states the selected public holiday applies to — AND that building has Public Holiday Applies? = Yes in buildings.csv
  4. Their email is not on the Do Not Send list

Column requirements:

people.csvName, Email, Role, Enabled (required) · Company, Type (shown in results)
buildings.csvCode, Name, Region (state), Public Holiday Applies? (Yes/No)
exportUsers.csvEmail, BuildingID (must match Code in buildings.csv), Building (name)
Do Not SendOne email address per row — these people are always excluded regardless of other criteria

Results are sorted alphabetically by name. Uncheck any row to exclude that person before loading into the mailer.
